About BRAOU TV

BRAOU TV is an online educational platform developed to support students in distance learning through digital technology. It allows learners to access educational content by using YouTube lessons uploaded on the university's official channel as well as live programs conducted regularly. Through this platform, students can watch recorded lectures, attend live classes and have access to ongoing activities as well as archives. The live programs also provide opportunities for interaction, where students can listen to expert discussions and interact with them.

BRAOU TV is an extension of the university's earlier television-based teaching system, where lessons were broadcast through channels like Doordarshan. Now, with the help of the internet, these programs have been transformed into a more flexible and accessible format. Students can learn at their own pace, pause and replay recorded videos, and stay updated with ongoing academic sessions through live streaming.

This platform plays a significant role in making education inclusive and convenient, especially for those who cannot attend regular classes due to work, distance, or personal reasons. By combining recorded YouTube lessons and live interactive sessions, BRAOU TV ensures continuous learning support, better understanding of concepts, and effective exam preparation, making it an important tool in digital learning.
